Catalan Cream

Catalan Cream is a traditional Catalan dessert made of rich custard flavored with cinnamon and lemon zest, topped with a crispy caramelized sugar layer. It’s similar to crème brûlée and served chilled.

Ingredients

  • 4 egg yolks
  • 100g sugar
  • 500ml milk
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk egg yolks with sugar and cornstarch.
  2. Heat the milk, cinnamon stick, and lemon zest in a saucepan until warm.
  3. Gradually add the warm milk to the egg mixture, whisking constantly.
  4. Pour the mixture back into the saucepan and cook over low heat until it thickens.
  5. Remove from heat, strain, and let cool.
  6. Pour into ramekins and refrigerate for a few hours.
  7. Before serving, sprinkle sugar on top and caramelize with a torch.
